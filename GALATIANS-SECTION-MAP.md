# Galatians section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Galatians, following the letter as it moves from the
greeting and Paul's alarm that they are turning to another gospel, through his
defense of the gospel he received and the argument that justification is by
faith and not works of the law, into the ethical call to walk by the Spirit,
and the closing appeal centered on the cross. This fixes the `order` and `slug`
for each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status:
done means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/galatians/`.
