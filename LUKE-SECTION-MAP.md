# Luke section map (movement level)

infancy narratives, the Galilean ministry, the long travel narrative toward
Jerusalem with its many parables, and the passion and resurrection. Larger
teaching scenes and parable clusters are kept coherent as single movements where
the text's structure warrants. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study
so they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the
JSON exists in `src/content/studies/luke/`.
