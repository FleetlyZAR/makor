sweeping Day of the LORD, through judgment on the surrounding nations and
Jerusalem, to the closing promise of restoration and rejoicing. This fixes the
`order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at a
time. Status: done means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/zephaniah/`.
