# John section map (movement level)

The planned studies for John, following the Gospel's literary seams: the
prologue, the Book of Signs with its discourses tied to each sign, the Upper
Room Discourse kept coherent across its farewell chapters, and the passion and
resurrection. The account of the woman caught in adultery is included in the
running text under standard versification so coverage stays continuous. This
fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as we generate
them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/john/`.
