# 2 Chronicles section map (movement level)

The first movements cover the reign of Solomon and the building of the temple, and the rest trace the kings of Judah one regnal account at a time, from the division of the kingdom under Rehoboam to the fall of Jerusalem and the decree of Cyrus.
