# Colossians section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Colossians, following the letter as it moves from
greeting, thanksgiving, and prayer into the great declaration of the supremacy
of Christ, then the warning against hollow human philosophy, and finally the
call to put on the new self, order the household, and pray, closing with
greetings. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable
as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/colossians/`.
