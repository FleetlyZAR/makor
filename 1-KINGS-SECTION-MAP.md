# 1 Kings section map (movement level)

succession and reign of Solomon (chapters 1 to 11), the division of the kingdom
(chapters 12 to 16), and the Elijah cycle set against the house of Ahab
(chapters 17 to 22). This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they
stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON
exists in `src/content/studies/1-kings/`.
