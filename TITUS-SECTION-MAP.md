# Titus section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Titus, following the letter as it moves from the
extended greeting, through the charge to appoint elders and silence false
teachers on Crete, into the instructions for sound doctrine across the
households and the call to good works grounded in grace, closing with final
directions and greetings. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so
they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON
exists in `src/content/studies/titus/`.
