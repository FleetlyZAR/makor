# 1 Corinthians section map (movement level)

The planned studies for 1 Corinthians, following the letter as it moves from
greeting and thanksgiving through Paul's responses to the reports of division
and immorality, then his answers to the church's questions on marriage, food,
worship, gifts, and love, and finally the great chapter on resurrection and
the closing arrangements. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so
they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON
exists in `src/content/studies/1-corinthians/`.
