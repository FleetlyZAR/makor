# 1 Timothy section map (movement level)

The planned studies for 1 Timothy, following the letter as it moves from
greeting and the charge against false teachers, through instructions on prayer
and worship and the qualifications for overseers and deacons, into directions
for Timothy's own conduct and the ordering of the household of God, closing with
the call to godliness with contentment and a final charge. This fixes the
`order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at
a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/1-timothy/`.
