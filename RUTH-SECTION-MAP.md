# Ruth section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Ruth, following the author's four-act structure of
scenes: the emptying in Moab and the return, the field of Boaz, the threshing
floor, and the redemption at the gate. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for
each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done
means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/ruth/`.

Six movements in all. Boundaries can be adjusted as we go; two studies never share an order.
