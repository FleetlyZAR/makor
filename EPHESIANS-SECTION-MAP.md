# Ephesians section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Ephesians, following the letter's clear two-part shape:
the doctrinal half that unfolds God's eternal plan of redemption and the one new
people made in Christ, and the practical half that calls the church to walk
worthy in unity, holiness, household order, and spiritual warfare. This fixes
the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as we generate them one
at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/ephesians/`.
