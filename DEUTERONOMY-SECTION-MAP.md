# Deuteronomy section map (movement level)

addresses on the plains of Moab, the historical review, the restatement of the
covenant and its statutes, the blessings and curses, and the closing acts of
Moses in his song, blessing, and death. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for
each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done
means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/deuteronomy/`.
