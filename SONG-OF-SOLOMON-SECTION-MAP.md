# Song of Solomon section map (movement level)

poem: the alternating voices of the bride, the bridegroom, and the friends
through longing, springtime invitation, the night searches, the wedding
procession, the garden of union, and the closing praise of love as strong as
death. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as
we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/song-of-solomon/`.
