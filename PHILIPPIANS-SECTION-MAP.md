# Philippians section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Philippians, following the letter as it moves from
greeting and thanksgiving through Paul's report on his imprisonment and the call
to a gospel-worthy, humble mind modeled on Christ, into the pursuit of knowing
Christ above all, and the closing exhortations to rejoice and the thanks for
their gift. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable
as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/philippians/`.
