# 2 Kings section map (movement level)

Elisha cycle and the wars of Israel (chapters 1 to 8), the purge of Jehu and the
kings of the divided monarchy (chapters 9 to 16), the fall of Samaria and the
reforms of Judah (chapters 17 to 23), and the final fall of Jerusalem (chapters
24 to 25). This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable
as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/2-kings/`.
