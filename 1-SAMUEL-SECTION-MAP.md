Samuel narrative and the ark (chapters 1 to 7), the rise and rejection of Saul
(chapters 8 to 15), and the rise of David alongside Saul's decline (chapters 16
to 31). This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as
we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/1-samuel/`.
