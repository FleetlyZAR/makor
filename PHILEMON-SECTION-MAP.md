# Philemon section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Philemon, a single-chapter letter divided by its flow of
appeal: the greeting, the thanksgiving for Philemon's love, the heart of the
letter as Paul pleads for the returning slave Onesimus, the personal pledge to
cover any debt, and the closing greetings. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for
each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done
means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/philemon/`.
