narrative of chapters 1 to 3, then the long sequence of judgment and mercy oracles
that runs through chapter 14. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so
they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON
exists in `src/content/studies/hosea/`.
