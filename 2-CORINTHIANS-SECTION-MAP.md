# 2 Corinthians section map (movement level)

greeting and comfort in affliction, through Paul's defense of his changed plans
and his ministry of the new covenant, into the appeal for the collection, and
finally the impassioned defense of his apostleship against the false apostles
before the closing warnings. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so
they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON
exists in `src/content/studies/2-corinthians/`.
