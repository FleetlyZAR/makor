# 1 Chronicles section map (movement level)

The planned studies for 1 Chronicles, following the book's literary seams. The long genealogies of chapters 1 to 9 are grouped by tribe into a handful of sensible movements rather than one per name, and the narrative that follows is divided by its scenes: the fall of Saul, David's rise and the ark, the Davidic covenant and wars, and the ordering of temple worship.
