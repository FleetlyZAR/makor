# 2 Timothy section map (movement level)

The planned studies for 2 Timothy, following the letter as it moves from
greeting and the call to guard the gospel without shame, through the charges to
endure as a soldier, workman, and vessel for honor, into the warning about the
last days and the summons to continue in the Scriptures, closing with Paul's
final charge and personal instructions. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for
each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done
means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/2-timothy/`.
