# 2 Thessalonians section map (movement level)

The planned studies for 2 Thessalonians, following the letter as it moves from
greeting and thanksgiving with its assurance of judgment at Christ's coming,
through the correction about the day of the Lord and the man of lawlessness, into
the call to stand firm and the warning against idleness, closing with a
benediction. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable
as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/2-thessalonians/`.
