# 1 Thessalonians section map (movement level)

greeting and thanksgiving for their faith, through Paul's account of his ministry
among them and his relief at Timothy's report, into the exhortations to holy
living and the teaching on the Lord's coming, closing with final instructions.
This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as we
generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/1-thessalonians/`.
