# Matthew section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Matthew, following the Gospel's literary seams: the
infancy narrative, the individual scenes and miracles, and the five great
teaching blocks (the Sermon on the Mount, the Mission Discourse, the Parables,
the Community Discourse, and the Olivet Discourse) which are kept coherent as
whole units or a few adjacent movements. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for
each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done
means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/matthew/`.
