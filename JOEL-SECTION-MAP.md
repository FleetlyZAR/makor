# Joel section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Joel, divided along the book's movement from the locust
plague to the Day of the LORD, the call to return, and the promised restoration
and judgment of the nations. Chapter divisions differ between the English and
Hebrew traditions; this map uses standard BSB English versification with three
chapters. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as
we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/joel/`.
