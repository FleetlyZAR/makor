# Acts section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Acts, following the book's literary seams: the founding
of the Jerusalem church, the spread through Judea and Samaria, the conversion of
Saul, the three missionary journeys of Paul, and the arrest, defenses, and
voyage that carry the gospel to Rome. Journey stages and key episodes are kept
coherent as single movements where the narrative warrants. This fixes the
`order` and `slug` for each study so they stay stable as we generate them one at
a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in `src/content/studies/acts/`.
