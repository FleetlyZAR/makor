# Mark section map (movement level)

The planned studies for Mark, following the Gospel's brisk literary seams: the
rapid succession of miracles, callings, controversies, and teaching scenes that
carry Jesus from Galilee to the cross. Longer units such as the parable chapter
and the Olivet Discourse are kept coherent, and the account runs to the standard
ending at 16:20. This fixes the `order` and `slug` for each study so they stay
stable as we generate them one at a time. Status: done means the JSON exists in
`src/content/studies/mark/`.
